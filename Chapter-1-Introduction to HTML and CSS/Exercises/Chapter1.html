FILIPINO SPAGHETTI RECIPE

INGRIDIENTS

  • 2 lbs. Spaghetti noodles
  • 1 lb. ground pork
  • 6 ounces luncheon meat minced
  • 4 pieces hotdogs or beef franks
  • 35 ounces Filipino Style Spaghetti Sauce
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 medium onion minced
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Cook the Spaghetti noodles with salt and oil, once the pasta is cooked transfer the pasta to a bowl.
  2. After adding oil in the pan turn the heat up.
  3. Once the pan is ready, saute the onion and garlic.
  4. After the onions are ready, add the ground pork or ground beef. Cook until the color turns light brown. Dont overcook it.
  5. Add the luncheon meat and hotdog after the ground pork is light brown. Stir and cook for about 2 to 3 minutes.
  6. Pour in the Spaghetti sauce and Stir. After stirring it, now let it boil. Cover and simmer for 30 minutes.
  7. Add salt or pepper if the taste isn't enough.
  8. Add the filipino style sauce and top it with the cheese.
  9. Serve your filipino style spaghetti and enjoy!
